Welcome to SofClo

Company Overview

SofClo is a cloud-native platform designed to support Software Asset Management (SAM) and FinOps programs at both strategic and operational levels. Unlike traditional SAM tools, which focus primarily on technical tracking and compliance, SofClo bridges the gap between operational management and strategic alignment. The platform helps SAM and FinOps managers overcome operational bottlenecks, communicate more effectively with stakeholders, and secure management buy-in for their programs. Through community- driven feedback and industry best practices, SofClo aims to continuously evolve to meet the growing needs of these fields.

Mission Statement

"To empower SAM and FinOps professionals with a framework, operational model, and strategies they need to manage software and financial operations effectively, communicate with stakeholders, and drive sustainable success."

Our Solution

SAM and FinOps managers often face challenges such as inefficiencies, lack of visibility, and difficulties in aligning operations with business strategy. SAM-tools don't address the full scope of program management, especially stakeholder engagement. SofClo solves this problem by offering a structured SaaS platform that brings a SAM framework and operational model and integrates it with planning and communication elements into one cohesive system. By using SofClo, teams can optimize their SAM and FinOps programs, reduce inefficiencies, and demonstrate value to leadership. The "SAM online Framework" SaaS tool is not there to replace all existing capabilities within SAM, but exists to support and increase the impact of existing and invested People, Process and Technology.
